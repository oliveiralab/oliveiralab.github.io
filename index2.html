Our Vision

At OliveiraLab, we envision a future where data-driven tools are seamlessly integrated into farming operations. Our goal is to co-create solutions with farmers and stakeholders that enhance decision-making, reduce environmental impact, and ensure long-term sustainability through digital transformation.

Our Research Focus

Innovative, Applied, and Impact-Driven

OliveiraLab integrates agronomy, AI, and geospatial tools to deliver digital agriculture solutions. Our work focuses on precision farming, irrigation optimization, yield prediction, crop maturity detection, and digital tools that improve efficiency, productivity, and sustainability. Through on-farm research and stakeholder collaboration, we develop actionable insights and empower both growers and extension professionals.

Recent Projects

Peanut Maturity via AI

Deep learning models and drone imagery to predict peanut maturity in real time using object detection and regression models.

Drone-Based Stand Count

Evaluating drone-based stand count tools across Nebraska to support early season management decisions for corn and soybeans.

Voice-Controlled Digital Ag Tools

Designing an AI assistant to help farmers interact with platforms like John Deere Operations Center via voice commands and computer vision.
