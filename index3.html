Our Vision

OliveiraLab envisions a future where data‑driven tools are seamlessly integrated into farming operations. We co‑create solutions with farmers and stakeholders that enhance decision‑making, reduce environmental impact, and promote resilience through digital transformation.

Research Themes

  • Precision Agriculture & Remote Sensing: High‑resolution drone and satellite imagery to monitor crop health and yield.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Decision Support: ML models for yield prediction, irrigation scheduling, and maturity estimation.
  • Farmer‑Centric Extension: On‑farm experiments, LLM tools, and hands‑on training that translate research into practice.

Highlighted Projects

AI‑Powered Peanut Maturity

YOLO object detection and multispectral UAV imagery to predict peanut maturity curves in real time.

Drone Stand Counts

Evaluating drone‑based stand‑count platforms across Nebraska for early-season corn & soybean management.

Voice AI for Irrigation

Building a voice‑controlled agent that connects to John Deere Operations Center and automates irrigation decisions.
